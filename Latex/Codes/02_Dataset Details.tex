\section{Dataset Details}

The dataset spans 25 years (2000–2024), with each row representing a year’s data for a particular cropping season. The following variables are included:
\begin{itemize}
    

\item  \textcolor{blue}{Year} – The year of observation
\item \textcolor{blue}{Rainfall (mm)} – Total seasonal rainfall
\item \textcolor{blue}{Avg Temperature (\degree C)} – Average seasonal temperature (in °C)
\item \textcolor{blue}{Fertilizer (kg/ha)} – Amount of fertilizer used (in kg per hectare)
\item \textcolor{blue}{Pest Incidents} – Number of major pest outbreaks during the season
\item \textcolor{blue}{Technology Index} – A composite score (0–100) indicating the level of technology adoption (e.g., mechanization, precision tools, improved seeds)
\item \textcolor{blue}{Crop Yield (ton/ha)} – Actual yield of the crop (in tons per hectare)

\end{itemize}
